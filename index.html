Our Products

At Eloheh Healthcare, we provide a selection of trusted prescription medications delivered directly to your home. Our licensed healthcare professionals ensure every order is safe, personalized, and reliable. Below you’ll find a list of medications we offer and their intended uses.

Medications Offered

  1. Semaglutide:

    Injectable used to manage type 2 diabetes and assist with weight loss in eligible individuals. It helps regulate blood sugar and improve insulin sensitivity.

  2. Metformin Tablets:

    Often the first medication prescribed for type 2 diabetes. It reduces glucose production in the liver and improves the body’s response to insulin.

  3. Insulin Medicine:

    Personalized insulin therapy to suit your body’s unique needs, helping maintain stable blood sugar levels throughout the day and night.

  4. Lisinopril:

    An ACE inhibitor that helps lower blood pressure and reduce strain on the heart, often used for treating hypertension and heart failure.

  5. Warfarin:

    A trusted anticoagulant that lowers the risk of blood clots, strokes, and heart attacks in high-risk patients.

  6. Atorvastatin:

    A statin medication that lowers LDL ("bad") cholesterol and supports the prevention of cardiovascular disease.

  7. Levothyroxine:

    A synthetic thyroid hormone used to treat hypothyroidism, restoring normal energy and metabolism by supplementing low hormone levels.

  8. Omeprazole:

    A widely used proton pump inhibitor (PPI) that reduces stomach acid and treats conditions like GERD, heartburn, and stomach ulcers.

  9. Azithromycin:

    An effective antibiotic used to treat infections such as bronchitis, pneumonia, sinus infections, and more.

  10. Amoxicillin Capsules:

    A broad-spectrum antibiotic that treats common infections of the ear, throat, urinary tract, and respiratory system.
