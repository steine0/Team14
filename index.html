What Makes Our Team Different

  • We blend clinical care and product delivery under one mission-driven team.
  • Our leadership is hands-on, community-focused, and patient-first.
  • All services are designed to be affordable, accessible, and high-quality.

Team Structure & Reporting Flow

  1. Department heads report directly to the CEO (Dr. Lena Harper).
  2. Team leads (e.g. nursing, supply chain, mental health) report to their department directors.
  3. Care providers, educators, and delivery staff report to team leads.
